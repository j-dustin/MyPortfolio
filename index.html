Projects

Network Infrastructure

When I was studying in University of Santo Tomas my team made a network infrastructure in Baesa Elementary School as our Capstone Project. We created a network topology catering to all the people attending the school by providing them internet connection all around the campus. We used GNS3 as a network simulation tool. Also, we made use of Windows Server 2022 to create users and Group Policy Objects within their computer lab. Lastly, we made use of pfSense for the firewall and to control what the users will be browsing in their browsers.
